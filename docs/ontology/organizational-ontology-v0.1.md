# Organizational Ontology v0.1

## Purpose

This ontology defines the conceptual entities, relationships, and organizational logic used in TatakelolaKampus.

The ontology functions as:
- AI reasoning foundation
- governance interpretation framework
- organizational sensing structure
- convergence-divergence analysis model

---

# Core Entities

## 1. Actor

Represents organizational actors such as:
- foundation
- rectorate
- dean
- vice dean
- lecturer
- staff
- student

### Actor Attributes
- role
- influence
- connectivity
- organizational orientation
- communication pattern

---

## 2. Interaction

Represents formal and informal organizational interactions.

Examples:
- meetings
- informal discussions
- complaints
- strategic dialogue
- spontaneous comments

### Interaction Attributes
- participants
- context
- communication tone
- emotional signals
- themes

---

## 3. Organizational Signal

Represents interpreted organizational meaning extracted from interactions.

Examples:
- trust signal
- burnout signal
- participation signal
- innovation signal
- conflict signal

---

## 4. Organizational Pulse

Represents dynamic organizational health indicators.

Core Pulse:
- trust pulse
- participation pulse
- energy pulse
- alignment pulse
- innovation pulse

---

## 5. Dynamics

Represents convergence-divergence patterns.

### Types
- Positive Convergence
- Constructive Divergence
- Destructive Divergence
- Negative Convergence

---

## 6. Governance Recommendation

Represents strategic recommendations generated by AI reasoning.

Examples:
- informal dialogue
- mediation
- participatory forum
- workload adjustment
- communication intervention

---

# Relationship Logic

Actor → Interaction → Signal → Pulse → Recommendation → Governance Action

---

# Foundational Principles

1. Every organizational expression has context.
2. Every organizational action contains intention.
3. Every organizational interaction contains problem-solving orientation.
4. Divergence is not always destructive.
5. Organizational health depends on dialogical continuity.
6. AI functions as organizational sensemaking assistant, not surveillance tool.
