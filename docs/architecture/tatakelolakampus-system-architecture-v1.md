# TatakelolaKampus System Architecture v1

## Overview

TatakelolaKampus is a reflective organizational intelligence system designed for higher education governance environments.

The system combines:
- organizational datasets,
- semantic ontology,
- governance reasoning,
- institutional pulse analysis,
- and adaptive interpretation models.

The architecture is designed to support:
- reflective governance,
- institutional learning,
- organizational diagnosis,
- and adaptive decision support.

---

# Core System Layers

## 1. Raw Interaction Layer

Purpose:
Capture unstructured organizational narratives.

Examples:
- meeting reflections,
- faculty discussions,
- institutional tensions,
- governance dialogues,
- administrative experiences.

Directory:
```text
datasets/raw-interactions/
```

---

## 2. Structured Semantic Layer

Purpose:
Transform organizational narratives into machine-readable semantic structures.

Includes:
- themes,
- actors,
- emotional signals,
- organizational intent,
- governance dynamics,
- institutional pulse indicators.

Directory:
```text
datasets/structured-interactions/
```

---

## 3. Organizational Ontology Layer

Purpose:
Provide semantic meaning structures for governance interpretation.

Includes:
- governance concepts,
- institutional dynamics,
- emotional-cultural taxonomy,
- signal classification,
- pulse dimensions.

Directory:
```text
datasets/organizational-ontology-v1.json
```

---

## 4. Sensemaking Prompt Layer

Purpose:
Guide organizational interpretation logic.

The prompt layer defines:
- reasoning philosophy,
- governance orientation,
- institutional interpretation principles,
- reflective analytical behavior.

Directory:
```text
prompts/
```

---

## 5. Institutional Pulse Engine

Purpose:
Estimate institutional conditions over time.

Pulse dimensions:
- trust_pulse,
- participation_pulse,
- innovation_pulse.

The pulse engine detects:
- institutional strain,
- adaptive recovery,
- governance fatigue,
- innovation health,
- legitimacy conditions.

---

## 6. Reflective Governance Layer

Purpose:
Generate governance-oriented institutional insights.

Outputs include:
- governance reflections,
- organizational diagnostics,
- adaptive recommendations,
- participation analysis,
- institutional learning insights.

---

# Analytical Flow

```text
Raw Narratives
    ↓
Structured Semantic Interpretation
    ↓
Ontology Mapping
    ↓
Sensemaking Reasoning
    ↓
Pulse Estimation
    ↓
Reflective Governance Insight
```

# Design Philosophy

TatakelolaKampus is NOT designed for:
- surveillance,
- punishment,
- authoritarian monitoring,
- political manipulation.

TatakelolaKampus IS designed for:
- institutional reflection,
- adaptive governance,
- organizational learning,
- participatory legitimacy,
- sustainable institutional development.
