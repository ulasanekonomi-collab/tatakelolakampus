# Governance Heatmap Engine v1

## Overview

The Governance Heatmap Engine is a visual institutional intelligence framework within TatakelolaKampus.

The engine visualizes:
- governance tensions,
- institutional trust conditions,
- participation dynamics,
- emotional-cultural signals,
- and adaptive organizational capacity across institutional areas.

The system supports:
reflective institutional awareness through spatial and relational governance visualization.

---

# Core Objectives

The Governance Heatmap Engine aims to:
- identify institutional hotspots,
- detect governance fragility zones,
- recognize adaptive governance clusters,
- visualize organizational stress distribution,
- support participatory institutional reflection.

---

# Visualization Philosophy

The Heatmap Engine is designed to:
- support reflection,
- strengthen institutional awareness,
- encourage dialogue,
- improve adaptive governance learning.

The system does NOT:
- rank individuals,
- expose personal vulnerability,
- justify punitive interventions.

Visualization focuses on:
institutional patterns rather than personal surveillance.

---

# Analytical Interpretation

Heatmap analysis should:
- identify recurring governance tensions,
- detect adaptive recovery areas,
- reveal institutional imbalance,
- support collaborative problem solving.

Heatmaps are:
interpretive governance tools,
not deterministic organizational judgments.

---

# Dynamic Temporal Heatmaps

The engine supports:
- longitudinal visualization,
- temporal pulse mapping,
- governance evolution tracking,
- adaptive recovery trajectories.

This enables:
institutional change observation over time.

---

# Ethical Constraints

The Governance Heatmap Engine must:
- preserve institutional dignity,
- anonymize sensitive information,
- avoid individual targeting,
- maintain contextual interpretation.

The system should strengthen:
ethical institutional reflection.
