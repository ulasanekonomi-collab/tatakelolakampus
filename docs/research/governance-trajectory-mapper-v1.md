# Governance Trajectory Mapper v1

## Overview

The Governance Trajectory Mapper is a longitudinal institutional intelligence framework within TatakelolaKampus.

The system analyzes:
- organizational pulse evolution,
- governance adaptation patterns,
- institutional tensions,
- emotional-cultural shifts,
- and resilience trajectories over time.

The Mapper identifies directional movement rather than static organizational conditions.

---

# Core Purpose

The Governance Trajectory Mapper aims to:

- detect governance evolution,
- estimate institutional movement patterns,
- identify emerging tensions,
- recognize adaptive recovery cycles,
- support long-term governance reflection.

---
